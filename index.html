Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the app "PlacesTracker" ("the App") handles user data.

The App is designed to function without requiring user accounts or external servers. Your privacy is important, and the App is built to minimize data collection.

2. Data Collection and Storage

The App does not collect or store personal data on external servers.

All data is stored locally on your device only, including:

  • Trips and activities
  • Locations (if added by the user)
  • Photos and videos selected by the user
  • Notes and timestamps

3. Permissions

Photos and Media

Used to select and import photos or videos into the App and display them within entries.

Location (Optional)

Used to allow users to select and store locations and display them on a map.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not automatically share any data with third parties.

Users may manually export and share data (e.g., via a .ptshare file) using external apps such as messaging or email services.

5. No Account Required

The App does not require registration, login, or cloud accounts.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use standard Android system components (such as file pickers or sharing features) which operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device. Security depends on your device protection (e.g., screen lock).

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not specifically target children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected on this page.
